Welcome to Hue

Hue is one of the few cities in Vietnam that has peaceful and romantic. When people talk about Hue, they usually think of “The imperial city.

ABOUT HUE

Hue was a political and cultural center of the Feudal State of Vietnam, this explains why there are hundreds of historical and cultural remains located in this area. On the other hand, the city cuisine is not only famous for the reasonable price but also for the variety and delicacy.

Once you enter Hue, you will see the way locals communicate with you. They always smile and talk with a soft voice, which makes them sound like they’re singing. Honestly, they are really friendly and always willing to help, this is a reason make us feel comfy.

A Travel Guide to Hue in Central Vietnam

Hues refined cuisine is the stuff of legend, and its leafy streets are lined with mossy pagodas, art déco mansions, and eye-popping markets. Through the whole scene flows the Perfume River, setting a languid pace the rest of the city is happy to follow.
